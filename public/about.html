About DashCore

DashCore is an all included HTML template, it's packed with multiple demos and tech stacks to help you get started with your project.

It includes multiple components and pre-made demos ready for you to personalize them according to your own needs. DashCore includes a variety of ready-to-go pages with many out-of-the-box features.
